General Intercessions for Conventual Mass

Introduction

To God the Father All Powerful, who wishes all men to be saved: through the intercession of Blessed Mary, Our Mother of Jasna Gora, with all the devotion of our minds, dear brothers, let us offer prayers:

    1. For the holy Church of God, that our Lord Jesus Christ might deign to guard, support and sanctify her with the power of His Spirit.
      We beseech you, hear us.
    2. For the Roman Pontiff N. that he might be able to carefully lead the flock entrusted to him by the Good Shepherd.
      We beseech you, hear us.
    1. For the primate of Poland, protector and confrater of our order, that the Mother of God of Jasna Gora might accompany him with her continual maternal protection.
      We beseech you, hear us.
    2. For our bishop N. and all clerics, that filled with the gift and spiritual grace of the Holy Spirit, they will preach to the Christian faithful not with infidelity but with submission to the Word of God.
      We beseech you, hear us.
    1. For our Pauline Order, that faithful to its vocation in contemplation and work it may be inflamed with love for the reign of the Virgin Mother of God.
      We beseech you, hear us.
    2. For our Pauline family, that by the Spirit of the Father, fraternal charity and the number of its members, by the intercession of Our Mother, might increase.
      We beseech you, hear us.
    3. For vocations to our religious family, that suitable young men moved by love towards the Mother of God, might frequently embrace our Order.
      We beseech you, hear us.
    1. For the Father General, that he might guide his companions according to the Divine will to obtain the final happiness.
      We beseech you, hear us.
    2. For the General Chapter of our Order, that the merciful God in his kindness might deign to grant his abundant blessings.
      We beseech you, hear us.
    1. For the novices and seminarians of our Order, that having persevered in the grace of a vocation, they grow in love of Christ and his Mother.
      We beseech you, hear us.
    2. For the confraters and benefactors of our Order, that the Mother and Patroness of our community might deign to repay them for the good done for us.
      We beseech you, hear us.
    3. For our deceased brothers, that called from their labours by the compassion of the Virgin Mother they might rest in eternal joy.
      We beseech you, hear us.
  6. For our church community, that the Lord might deign to admit us all as “a sacrifice acceptable to him” to the heavenly banquet.
    We beseech you, hear us.
Conclusion

By our prayers, we seek, Lord, that your ears graciously hear our devotions and humble prayers. Through Christ Our Lord. Amen.

Or

Merciful and compassionate God, grant to us through Mary, your Mother and our Patroness, all good which is in accord with your will and our salvation. Through Christ Our Lord. Amen.
