August 26
Solemnity of Our Lady of Częstochowa

Solemnity

Władysław, The Duke of Opole, built the monastery of Jasna Góra, for the Pauline Order and entrusted to their care the Icon of Our Lady venerated at his castle. Quickly the icon was renowned for being miraculous. Its fame increased greatly following the defence of Jasna Góra from the Swedes. In the year 1717, the icon was solemnly crowned and Jasna Góra became the chief centre of devotion to Our Lady in Poland.

Entrance Antiphon Ps 86(87):1-2

On the holy mountain is his city
cherished by the Lord.
The Lord prefers the gates of Sion
to all Jacob's dwellings.

The Gloria in excelsis (Glory to God in the highest) is said.

Collect

O Almighty and Merciful God,
you have wondrously established the most Blessed Virgin Mary
to be the perpetual help and defence of the Christian people,
and made her holy image at Jasna Gora renowned by the solemn veneration of the faithful.
Graciously grant that defended by her protection
as we fight the battle of life,
may we be victorious over the evil spirits at the hour of our death.
Through our Lord Jesus Christ, Your Son,
who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit,
one God for ever and ever.

Readings

The Creed is said.

Prayer over the Offerings

O Lord, we offer you these sacrificial gifts
to you for the triumph of the Christian religion.
May they be beneficial to us through the manifest help of the Virgin,
by whom was once achieved such a victory.
Through Christ our Lord.

Communion Antiphon Ps 86(87):3, 2

Of you are told glorious things,
O city of God!
The Lord prefers the gates of Sion
to all Jacob's dwellings.

Prayer after Communion

O Lord, stay close to the people
who are refreshed by sharing your Body and Blood.
Through the help of your most holy Mother,
may they be freed from every evil and danger
and protected in every good work.
Through Christ our Lord.
