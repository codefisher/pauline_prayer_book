November 3
Commemoration of all the Departed of the Order

Memorial

Entrance Antiphon Cf. Rev 14:13

Blessed are the dead who die in the Lord.
Henceforth may they rest from their labours:
for their deeds follow them.

Collect

O God, glory of the faithful and life of the just,
by the Death and Resurrection of whose Son
we have been redeemed,
look mercifully on your departed servants,
the brothers, relatives and benefactors of our Order,
that, just as they professed the mystery of our resurrection,
so they may merit to receive the joys of eternal happiness.
Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son,
who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit,
one God, for ever and ever.

Prayer over the Offerings

O God, whose mercy is without limit,
graciously receive our humble prayers
and by these gifts which we offer to you
grant remission from every sin
to the souls of the brothers, relatives and benefactors
of our Order of the First Hermit.
Through Christ our Lord.

Communion Antiphon

We receive the Body and Blood of Christ in memory of the brothers, relatives and benefactors of our Order; O Lord give them eternal rest.

Prayer after Communion

Almighty and merciful God, grant, we beseech you,
that the souls of the brothers, relatives and benefactors of our Order,
for whom we have offered this sacrifice of praise to your majesty,
may, purified from every sin by the power of this sacrament,
receive the joy of the eternal light.
Through Christ our Lord.
