November 3
Commemoration of all the Departed of the Order

Memorial

Entrance Antiphon Cf. Rev 14:13

Blessed are the dead who have died in the Lord.
Henceforth may they rest from their labours:
for their deeds follow them.

Collect

O God, glory of the faithful and life of the just,
by the death and resurrection of whose son we are redeemed,
be favorable to your servants the departed brothers, confraters and benefactors of our Order,
so that they who have professed the mystery of your resurrection
may merit to receive the joys of eternal happiness.
Through our Lord Jesus Christ, Your Son,
who lives and reigns with You in the unity of the Holy Spirit,
one God for ever and ever.

Prayer over the Offerings

O God, whose mercy is without limit,
graciously receive the prayers of our humility
and by these gifts which we offer to you
grant remission from every sin to the
souls of the brothers, confraters and benefactors of our Order of the First Hermit.
Through Christ our Lord.

Communion Antiphon

We receive the Body and Blood of Christ in memory of the brother, confraters and benefactors of our Order; O Lord give them eternal rest.

Prayer after Communion

Almighty and merciful God, grant we beseech you,
that the souls of the brother, confraters and benefactors of our Order,
for whom we have offered this sacrifice of praise to your majesty,
may purified from every sin by your mercy,
receive the beatitude of eternal light.
Through Christ our Lord.
