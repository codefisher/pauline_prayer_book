July 2
Blessed Virgin Mary of
Leśniów

Solemnity

Entrance Antiphon Lc 1, 42

Blessed are you among women and blessed is the fruit of your womb.

The Gloria in excelsis (Glory to God in the highest) is said.

Collect

God, who though your only begotten Son,
elevated in holiness home life,
grant, we ask, that our family,
supported by the patronage of the blessed Virgin Mary,
might become a home of the church,
a communion marked by life and love.
Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son,
who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit,
one God, for ever and ever.

Readings

On the Solemnity: The Creed is said.

Prayer over the Offerings

Accept, we pray, O Lord,
the sacrifice of conciliation and grant
that our family, by the intercession Mother of God,
overflow with grace, peace and prosperity.
Through Christ our Lord.
Preface I of the Blessed Virgin Mary: on the Solemnity
(for a votive Mass: in veneration)

Communion Antiphon Lk 1:28

He looks on his servant in her lowliness;
henceforth all ages will call me blessed.

Prayer after Communion

Having been nourished by Body and Blood of your Son on this solemnity
(commeneration) of the blessed Mary ever Virgin
to you, Lord, we pray, that through her intercession,
our family may go forward on the way of salvation,
and be lead to the eternal pasture.
Through Christ our Lord.
