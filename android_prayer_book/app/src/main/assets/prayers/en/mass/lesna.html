September 26
Blessed Virgin Mary of
Lesna

Solemnity

Entrance Antiphon Ps 102(103), 1-2

My soul, give thanks to the Lord, all my being bless his holy name.

The Gloria in excelsis (Glory to God in the highest) is said.

Collect

All powerful God,
who gave to us the most blessed Virgin Mary
as mother of faith and unity,
grant, we pray, that by her example,
we may humbly grasp the gifts of the Holy Spirit,
and daily enhance our christian life.
Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son,
who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit,
one God, for ever and ever.

Readings

On the Solemnity: The Creed is said.

Prayer over the Offerings

Look upon, Lord, the gifts offered and presented to you,
that through the intercession of the blessed Mary ever Virgin,
our Mother and Patron,
we may be able to hold in abundance the fruits of the Holy Spirit.
Through Christ our Lord.
Preface I of the Blessed Virgin Mary: on the Solemnity
(for a votive Mass: in veneration)

Communion Antiphon Lk 1:42

Blessed are you among women
and blessed is the fruit of your womb.

Prayer after Communion

Having been made new by heavenly nourishment,
we ask, Lord God,
that, through the intercession of the blessed Virgin Mary,
we be able to achieve a community of joy
with you and the Church.
Through Christ our Lord.
