June 8
St Thecla, Virgin and Martyr

Optional Memorial

From the Common of Martyrs: For a Virgin Martyr, or from the Common of Virgins: For One Virgin

Collect

O God, for the glory of your name blessed Thecla, virgin and martyr,
withstood the great contest of sufferings
with her faith unconquered,
grant to us in imitation of the her faith
to despise the success of the world
and to tremble at none of its adversity.
Through our Lord Jesus Christ your Son,
who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit,
one God, for ever and ever.
