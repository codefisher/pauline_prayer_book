Solemnity of St. Paul the First Hermit Blessing of Fruits

Introduction to the blessing of fruits of the palm:

God makes known his mighty power and his abounding goodness to all the ends of the earth; but he also directs his Church to bless the simplest and most commonplace things. He does so in order that all who devoutly make use of material things, while invoking St. Paul the First Hermit may be drawn to the world that is not seen and may give glory to God, who alone performs the wonders we call miracles, but who is wonderful also in his saints.

Prayer of blessing:

Let us pray: Lord God, You beautify your Church with the rich variety of the virtues of your saints, and through the intercession of St. Paul the First Hermit, grant that as we eat the fruit of the palm brought here for your blessing, we may be eager to imitate St. Paul the First hermit whose life we celebrate, and that our reward in heaven may be the company of the Blessed Virgin Mary, St. Paul the First Hermit and all the Saints, whose protection is our comfort on earth. Through Christ our Lord. Amen.
