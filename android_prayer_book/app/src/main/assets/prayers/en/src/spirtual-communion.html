3. An Act of Spiritual Communion

O Lord, Jesus Christ, truly present in the Holy Sacrament, I believe in you, I adore you and I love you with all my heart. I am sorry for all my sins, because I desire to receive you in Holy Com- munion and remain with you. Come, O Jesus to my heart, spiritu- ally at least and dwell in it forever…

May the Body of our Lord Jesus Christ guard me for life everlasting.

I welcome you, Jesus into my poor heart. I dedicate myself totally to you. I am yours in life and in death. Amen.
