Morning Prayer

Giver of life, eternal Lord, Thy own redeemed defend; Mother of grace, thy children save, and help them to the end. Army of Martyrs, holy Priests, in beautiful array; Ye happy troops of Virgins chaste, wash all our stains away. All ye who high above the stars in heavenly glory reign, May we through your prevailing prayers unto your joys attain. Praise, honor, to the Father be, praise to His only Son; Praise, Holy Paraclete, to Thee, while endless ages run.

Psalm 62 (63)

O God, you are my God, for you I long; * - for you my soul is thirsting. My body pines for you * - like a dry, weary land without water. So I gaze on you in the sanctuary * - to see your strength and your glory. For your love is better than life, * - my lips will speak your praise. So I will bless you all my life, * - in your name I will lift up my hands. My soul shall be filled as with a banquet, * - my mouth shall praise you with joy. On my bed I remember you. * - On you I muse through the night for you have been my help; * - in the shadow of your wings I rejoice. My soul clings to you; * - your right hand holds me fast.

Blessed be the Lord, the God of Israel! * - He has visited his people and redeemed them. He has raised up for us a mighty saviour * - in the house of David his servant, as he promised by the lips of holy men, * - those who were his prophets from of old. A saviour who would free us from our foes, * - from the hands of all who hate us. So his love for our fathers is fulfilled * - and his holy covenant remembered. He swore to Abraham our father to grant us, * - that free from fear, and saved from the hands of our foes, we might serve him in holiness and justice * - all the days of our life in his presence. As for you, little child, * - you shall be called a prophet of God, the Most High. You shall go ahead of the Lord * - to prepare his ways before him, To make known to his people their salvation * - through forgiveness of all their sins, the loving-kindness of the heart of our God * - who visits us like the dawn from on high. He will give light to those in darkness, + - those who dwell in the shadow of death, * - and guide us into the way of peace.

R/ Through the intercession of the saints save us, Lord. God, font of sanctity, — grant to us in this, your greatness, to celebrate. R/ Most provident and eternal God, who shows to us the image of your Son in the saints, — grant, that we might come to union with Christ through their efforts. R/ King of heaven, who through faithful followers of Christ hasten the coming of the future city, — grant that we might learn from them down the safer road that leads to it. Our Father…

Almighty ever-living God, who ever adorn, protect and keep your church with the different splendours of the saints, graciously grant that by the prayers of blessed Paul the First Hermit and his holy sons and daughters, the merits of all of whom you have granted us to honour on one feast day, both that we may be purified from every fault and that we may fully enjoy eternal glory in the future. who lives and reigns with You in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God for ever and ever.
