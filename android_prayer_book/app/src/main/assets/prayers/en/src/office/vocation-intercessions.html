Lauds

Intercessions – for Priestly and Religious Vocations

Jesus, Divine Shepard, you called the Apostles and made them into fishers of men. — Likewise, today, call the young to your service in our Order. R/ O, Jesus, that the saving administration of the Sacraments may be given to us, — We as you, give us Holy Priests. R/ Jesus, Divine Shepard, we ask you for new Priestly and Brotherly vocations to our Order, — Grant that the World will never lack good and courageous men, who will answer the voice of the Divine Call. R/ O God, endow your Church with numerous vocations to the priesthood, — So that there never will be a lack of those who proclaim the good news and serve others with charity. R/ O God, Shepard and teacher of the faithful, who for the safeguarding and growth of your church established the Priesthood, deign to send workers into your field — Send worthy priests, especially to our community. R/ You call the baptised to set out into the deep, walking in the ways of holiness. — Awaken in the hearts of the young a desire to be in the modern world true witness of the power of your love. R/

Vespers

Intercessions – for Priestly and Religious Vocations

Father, grant, that among Christians will blossom many holy priestly and religious vocations, especially for our community, — Who will sustain the living faith in the hearts of men. R/ Call servants of your mercy, — Who through the sacrament of reconciliation will share the joy of your forgiveness. R/ O Jesus, for the forgiveness of ours sins in the sacrament of penance and reconciliation, — We ask you, send us Holy Priests. R/ O Jesus, Divine Shepard, who called the apostles, so that you could make them fishers of souls, — Draw to yourself the zealous and noble thoughts of young men and make them your servants in our Order. R/ Son of God, we beseech you, through the intercession of Our Lady, the formator of priestly vocations, — Grant, that in our Order there will never be a lack of vocations, for the dedicated service to your kingdom. R/ Lord, You are the good shepherd, we ask you, — Endow young men with an openness to your call. R/
