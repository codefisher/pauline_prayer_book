Prayer During the Day

Before Noon

Be consecrated to me, for I, the Lord, am holy, and shall set you apart from all these peoples, for you to be mine.

R/ I am with your always,
V/ Take my right hand.

Midday

What does the Lord your God ask of you? Only this: to fear the Lord your God, to follow all his ways, to love him, to serve the Lord your God with all your heart and all your soul.

R/ As God's dear children take him as your pattern,
V/ And follow Christ in loving.

Afternoon

Brother, we wish you joy; try to grow perfect; encourage one another; have a common mind and live in peace, and the God of love and peace will be with you.

R/ As servants of God, honour everyone,
V/ Love your fellow-believers, fear God.

O God, who called the Blessed Eusebius into the wilderness of Pilis out of love towards you, that there he might offer his life in propitiation for the people and gather the scattered hermits, grant, we beseech you, that for the sake of his life and intercession our order may be made one in faith and love. Through our Lord Jesus Christ your Son, who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.
