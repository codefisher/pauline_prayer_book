January 19
Saint Macarius the Great, Abbot

Optional Memorial

Macarius was born in Alexandria circa AD 300. In his youth, he went to the desert near Thebes, where he spent 29 years. Next, he moved to the Scetic Desert, where he gave himself to an even more rigorously penitential life. He was then ordained a priest. Living in prayer and fasting, he received the gift of prophecy as well the ability to work miracles.

From the Common of pastors: For One Pastor, or from the Common of Holy Men and Women: For Monks and Religious, (for an abbot).

Collect

O God, almighty Father, grant we beseech you, that we too imitate the life of the Abbot blessed Macarius, we may be made worthy of your supreme love and may receive with him a like reward from the everlasting spring of our Saviour Jesus Christ. Who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.
