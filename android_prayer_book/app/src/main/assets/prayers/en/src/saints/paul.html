January 15
Saint Paul, The First Hermit,
Father of the Order

Solemnity

Paul was born around AD 228 in Thebes. When he was sixteen, the persecutions of Christians began. Hiding in the desert, he resolved to spend the rest of his life in solitude and in prayer.
