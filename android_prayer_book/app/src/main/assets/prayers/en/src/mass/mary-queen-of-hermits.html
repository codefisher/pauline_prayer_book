January 16
Feast of
The Blessed Virgin Mary,
Queen of Hermits and Patroness of the Order

Feast

Entrance Antiphon Is 35:1-2

Let the wilderness and the dry-lands exult, let the wasteland rejoice and bloom, let it bring forth flowers like the jonquil.

The Gloria in excelsis (Glory to God in the highest) is said.

Collect

Almighty God, who granted that our Order be governed under the patronage of the Immaculate Virgin Mary and Queen of Hermits, that we may faithfully follow the examples of her very life hidden in you with Christ. Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

Readings • Renewal of the Covenant with Mary

Prayer over the Offerings

Receive, Lord, the gifts we have offered you, and grant that the blessed Virgin Mary, Queen of Hermits and Mother of our Order, may help us to fulfil our vocation and mission in the Church. Through Christ our Lord.

Preface

V/ The Lord be with you.
R/ And with your spirit.

V/ Lift up your hearts.
R/ We lift them up to the Lord.

V/ Let us give thanks to the Lord our God.
R/ It is right and just.

It is truly right and just, our duty and our salvation, always and everywhere to give you thanks, Lord, holy Father, almighty and eternal God, on this commemoration of the Virgin Mary Queen of Hermits. For she is that Virgin who obediently in the silence of her heart received with spousal love the incarnation of your Son. Overflowing with this same grace, she visited Elizabeth, in whose womb she caused John, “the voice of one crying in the desert,” to leap for joy. Moreover, when in Egypt with the pursued Christ, she traversed the desert as an exile. Within the home of the family of Nazareth she lived in silent contemplation meditating in her own soul the wisdom of Jesus. But in the hour of her greatest desolation when she stood at the foot of the cross of your Son, by the strength of your Spirit she confirmed her role, her motherhood of the Church. For this reason, holy Father, by your gift we call her Queen and Mother, so that our Order may always be an equally faithful witness to your son. Through him the host of Angels adores your majesty and rejoices in your presence for ever. May our voices, we pray, join with theirs in one chorus of exultant praise, as we acclaim: Holy, Holy, Holy Lord God of hosts. Heaven and earth are full of your glory. Hosanna in the highest. Blessed is he who comes in the name of the Lord. Hosanna in the highest.

Communion Antiphon Sir 24:21-22

They who eat me will hunger for more, they who drink me will thirst for more. Whoever listens to me will never have to blush, whoever acts as I dictate will never sin.

Prayer after Communion

Lord, grant that we, nourished by the springs of salvation, and supported by the power of this Sacrament and the intercession of our Mother and Queen, may serve more faithfully day by day the mystery of our redemption. Through Christ our Lord.
