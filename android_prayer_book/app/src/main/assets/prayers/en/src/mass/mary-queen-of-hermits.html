January 16
Feast of
The Most Holy Virgin Mary,
Queen of Hermits and Patroness of the Order

Solemnity

Entrance Antiphon Is 35:1-2

Let the wilderness and the dry-lands exult, let the wasteland rejoice and bloom, let it bring forth flowers like the jonquil, let it rejoice and sing for joy. The glory of Lebanon is bestowed on it, the splendour of Carmel and Sharon;

Collect

Almighty God, who gave our Order under the special care and rule of the Immaculate Virgin Mary and Queen of Hermits, grant us the grace to faithfully imitate her hidden life with Christ in God. Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

Readings • Renewal of the Covenant with Mary

Prayer over the Offerings

Receive Lord, these gifts offered to you, and may the support of Our Lady Queen of Hermits and the Mother of our Order, help us to fulfil our vocation and mission in the Church. Through Christ our Lord.

Preface

V/ The Lord be with you.
R/ And with your spirit.

V/ Left up your hearts.
R/ We lift them up to the Lord.

V/ Let us give thanks to the Lord our God.
R/ It is right and just.

It is truly right and just, our duty and our salvation, always and everywhere to give you thanks, Lord, holy Father, almighty and eternal God, on this commemoration of the Virgin Mary Queen of Hermits. For she herself is the Virgin who listens who in the silence of her heart received with spousal love the incarnation of your Son. Overflowing with this same grace she visited Elizabeth in whose womb John, “the voice of one crying in the desert,” was made to leap for joy. Moreover when in Egypt with the pursued Christ she fled as an exile to the desert. Within the home of the family of Nazareth she lived in silent contemplation meditating in her own soul the wisdom of Jesus. But in the hour of her greatest desolation when she stood at the foot of the cross of your Son, by the strength of your Spirit she confirmed the mission of her maternal protection of the Church????. For this reason, holy Father, by your gift we call her Queen and Mother, that our Order might always be an equally faithful witness to your son. Through him the host of Angles adores your majesty and rejoices in your presence for ever. May our voices, we pray, join with theirs, in one chorus of exultant praise, as we acclaim: Holy, Holy, Holy Lord God of hosts. Heaven and earth are full of your glory. Hosanna in the highest. Blessed is he who comes in the name of the Lord. Hosanna in the highest.

Communion Antiphon Sir 24:21-22

They who eat me will hunger for more, they who drink me will thirst for more. Whoever listens to me will never have to blush, whoever acts as I dictate will never sin.

Prayer after Communion

Lord God, grant that we who are nourished from the springs of Salvation, supported by the power of this Sacrament and the intercession of Our Mother and Queen, may serve more and more faithfully the mystery of Redemption. Through Christ our Lord.
