June 8
Saint Hedwig, Queen

Optional Memorial

Hedwig, born in the year 1374, was the daughter of King Louis, the king of Hungary and Poland. From her father she inherited the Polish Throne and was crowned in 1384. Marrying the Grand Duke of Lithuania, Władysław Jagiełło, she contributed to the conversion of Lithuania to Christianity. She supported the creation of the Krakow Theological Institute as well as the renewal of the university. Her entire life, she was marked by generosity to the poor. She died in Krakow on July 17, 1399.
