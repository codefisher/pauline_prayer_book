January 20
Blessed Eusebius of Esztergom,
Priest, Founder of the Order

Feast

Eusebius was born around 1200. He received Priestly orders and was made a Canon of the Cathedral of Esztergom. In the year 1216, after receiving the permission of his bishop, he began to live an eremitical life in the Pilis Mountains. In the year 1246, he renounced his Canonry, and lived with brother hermits near Pilisszántó. He founded a monastic community under the patronage of the Holy Cross. In the year 1256, the community elected him as the first Provincial of the Pauline Order.

Entrance Antiphon Cf. Ps 23(24):5-6

These are the holy ones - who have received a blessing from the Lord, and mercy from the God who saves them, - because this is the generation of those who seek the Lord.

The Gloria in excelsis (Glory to God in the highest) is said.

Collect

O God, who called Blessed Eusebius into the wilderness of Pilis out of his love for you, that there he might offer his life in propitiation for the people and gather the scattered hermits, grant, we beseech you, that through his life and intercession our order may be made one in faith and love. Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

Readings • General Intercessions

Prayer over the Offerings

O God, who in the soul of Blessed Eusebius created a new man in your image in place of the old man, grant that we too, with soul renewed, may offer to you this sacrifice of praise. Through Christ our Lord.

Communion Antiphon Cf. Mt 19:27-29

Amen, I say to you, that you who have left all and followed me will receive a hundredfold and possess eternal life.

Prayer after Communion

O God, who chose Blessed Eusebius through the fire of your grace that he might increase your Church with a new community loving God, grant that, strengthened through Holy Communion, and imitating him in loving you above all, we may serve the salvation of those nearest us. Through Christ our Lord.
