January 20
Blessed Eusebius,
Priest, Founder of the Order

Feast

Entrance Antiphon Cf. Ps 23:5-6

These are the holy ones, - who have received blesings from the Lord, and mercy from the God who saves them, such is the people who seek the Lord.

The Gloria in excelsis (Glory to God in the highest) is said.

Collect

O God, who called the Blessed Eusebius into the wilderness of Pilis out of love towards you, that there he might offer his life in propitiation for the people and gather the scattered hermits, grant, we beseech you, that for the sake of his life and intercession our order may be made one in faith and love. Through our Lord Jesus Christ your Son, who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

Prayer over the Offerings

O God who in the soul of Blessed Eusebius created a new man in your image in place of the old man, grant that from a renewed soul we may offer to you this sacrifice of praise. Through Christ our Lord.

Communion Antiphon Cf. Mt 19:27-29

Amen, I say to you, that you who have left all and followed me will receive a hundredfold and possess eternal life.

Prayer after Communion

O God, who chose Blessed Eusebius through the fire of your grace that he might increase your church with a new community loving God, grant that strengthened through holy communion we may imitate him in loving you above all, and serve the salvation of our neighbours. Through Christ our Lord.
