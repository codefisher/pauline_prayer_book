Save us
Blood of Christ, incarnate Word of God,
Blood of Christ, of the New and Eternal Covenant,
Blood of Christ, falling upon the earth in Agony,
Blood of Christ, shed profusely in the Scourging,
Blood of Christ, flowing forth in the Crowning with thorns,

Blood of Jesus, Courage of Martyrs,
Blood of Jesus, Strength of Confessors,
Blood of Jesus, bringing forth virgins,
Blood of Jesus, Help of those in peril,
Blood of Jesus, Relief of the burdened,
Blood of Jesus, Solace in sorrow,
Blood of Jesus, Hope of the penitent,
Blood of Jesus, Consolation of the dying,
Blood of Jesus, Peace and Tenderness of our hearts,
Blood of Jesus, Pledge of Eternal Life,
Blood of Jesus, freeing souls from Purgatory,
Blood of Jesus, most worthy of all glory and honour,
Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world,
Spare us, O Lord.
Lamb of God, you take away the sins of the world,
Graciously hear us, O Lord.

Have mercy on us.
L.
You have redeemed us, O Lord, in your Blood.
All
And made of us a kingdom for our God.
L.
Let us pray:
Almighty and Eternal God, you have appointed your onlybegotten Son the Redeemer of the world, and willed to be appeased by his Blood. Grant, we beseech you, that we may worthily adore this Price of our salvation, and through its power, be safeguarded from the evils of this present life, so that we may rejoice in tis fruits forever in Heaven. Through the same Christ Our Lord.
All
Amen.

They have pierced my hands and my feet.
All
They have counted all my bones.

Let us pray:
Almighty and Eternal God, out of obedience, your Son, Jesus Christ, hung upon the Cross for our salvation.
