“From the very beginning, our Order has enjoyed the special assistance of Mary. She has always been present in the life and activity of the Pauline Community. Our Order has always honoured her as Mother, Lady and Queen and has striven to propagate (art. 60) special devotion to the Mother of God; a devotion which expresses itself principally in the imitation of Mary, in the action of one’s personal sanctification and in the carrying out of apostolic works.” (art. 3)

Almighty God, look kindly upon the prayers your servants offer you in honour of the Most Holy Virgin Mary, the Mother of God, and in your mercy grant us your help. Through Christ Our Lord. Amen
