“It is our conviction that in the course of some very turbu- lent history associated with our Order (of which there is no lack), the protection of Mary has been a support in striving for holiness and making effective our mission to those peoples to whom Divine Providence has sent us” (art. 60).

O, Lord, our God, may the intercession of the Most Holy Virgin Mary protect us always, that freed from all dangers, we may rejoice in your peace. Through Christ our Lord. Amen.

A painting, produced in the workshop of Jasna Gora, XVII century. From the Art collections of Jasna Gora.
