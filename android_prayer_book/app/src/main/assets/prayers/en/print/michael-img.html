“At that time Michael will stand up, the great prince, who mounts guard over your people” (Dn 12: 1).

O God, who arranges the services of Angels and men in a wonderful order; mercifully grant that our life may be protected on earth by those who always do your service in heaven. Through our Lord, Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you, in the unity of the Holy Spirit, One God, for ever and ever. Amen.
