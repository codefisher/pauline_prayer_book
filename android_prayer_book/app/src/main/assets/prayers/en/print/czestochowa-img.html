“In the sixth month, the Angel Gabriel was sent by God to a town in Galilee, called Nazareth, to a Virgin betrothed to a man named Joseph, of the House of David; and the Virgin’s name was Mary” (Lk 1:26-27)

Lord, through you will the Eternal Word became incarnate in the womb of the Virgin Mary, grant that we who have confessed the Saviour to be true God and True Man, may merit a participation in His Divine Nature, who lives and reigns with you and the Holy Spirit, one God for ever and ever. Amen.
