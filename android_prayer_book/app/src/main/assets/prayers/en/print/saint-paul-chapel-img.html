“But because of Christ, I have come to consider all these advantages that I had as disadvantages. Not only that, but I believe nothing can happen that will outweigh the supreme advantage of knowing Christ Jesus my Lord “(Ph 3 7-8).

St. Paul, who now enjoys the bliss of Heaven, Kindly look down on your sons here below. Draw them to yourself, By the power of your prayer.

The chapel of St. Paul the Hermit, a work of Franciszek Zaor; Ba- silica of Jasna Gora, XVII century.
