June 8
St Hedwig, Queen

Optional Memorial

From the Common of Holy Men and Women: For Holy Women.

The Office of Readings

Second Reading

Cf. 1 Cor. 13:13, 1 John 3:14. 16
Responsory

R. The greatest is love; * The one who loves, partakes of the life which is from God.
V. In this we know love: Christ gave up his life for us. * The one who loves, partakes of the life which is from God.

The concluding prayer as at Morning Prayer.

Morning Prayer

Benedictus ant. Today rejoice, O Saint Queen Hedwig, in the name of all the nations of which you are the mother of the faith.

Concluding Prayer
O God, who gave to St Hedwig
the gift of love and contemplation of Christ crucified,
that she might wisely serve him with love for her brothers,
grant even to us that by her intercession
we may love you with unwearied love in prayer and in works.
Through our Lord Jesus Christ your Son,
who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit,
one God, for ever and ever.

Evening Prayer

Magnificat ant. We praise you, O Saint Queen Hedwig, who in your life showed love is the highest of all virtue.
