A. Daily Prayers

1. Morning Prayers

L.
In the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Spirit.
All
Amen.
L.
Eternal Father
All
In union with the intentions of your Son, Jesus Christ, animated by the power of the Holy Spirit, calling upon the intercession of the Blessed Virgin Mary, St. Paul, Patriarch of our Order and all the Saints, we offer you all our thoughts, words and actions of this day for your greater glory, for our sanctification and for the salvation of the world.

Ordinary Time

L.
The Angel of the Lord declared unto Mary,
All
And she conceived of the Holy Spirit

Hail Mary, full of grace, the Lord is with thee; blessed art thou among women, and blessed is the fruit of thy womb, Jesus.

Holy Mary, Mother of God, pray for us sinners, now and at the hour of our death. Amen.

L.
Behold the handmaid of the Lord,
All
Be it done unto me according to your word.

Hail Mary …

L.
And the Word was made flesh,
All
And dwelt among us.

Hail Mary …

L.
Pray for us, O Holy Mother of God.
All
That we may be made worthy of the promises of Christ.
L.
Let us pray:
Pour forth, we beseech You, O Lord, Your grace into our hearts, that we, to whom the Incarnation of Christ, Your Son, was made known by the message of an Angel; may by His Passion and Cross be brought to the glory of His Resurrection. Through the same Christ our Lord.
All
Amen.

Easter

From Easter Sunday to the solemnity of Pentecost instead of the "Angelus" we recite the following antiphon:

L.
Queen of heaven, rejoice, alleluia.
All
For he, whom you were worthy to bear, alleluia,
has risen, as He said, alleluia.
Pray for us to God, alleluia.
L.
Rejoice and be glad, O Virgin Mary, alleluia.
All
For the Lord has truly risen, alleluia.
L.
Let us pray:
God our Father, you have given joy to the world by the resurrection of your Son Jesus Christ. Through the prayers of his Mother, the Virgin Mary, bring us to the happiness of eternal life. We ask this through Christ our Lord.
All
Amen.
L.
Glory be to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit.
All
As it was in the beginning, is now, and ever shall be world without end. Amen.
L.
Eternal rest grant unto them, O Lord.
All
And let perpetual light shine upon them.
L.
May they rest in peace.
All
Amen.

Ordinary Time

L.
Rejoice, O Mother of God, Immaculate Virgin
All
Rejoice, you who received glad tidings from an Angel,
Rejoice, you who gave birth to the Brightness of the Eternal Light,
Rejoice, O Most Holy Mother of God.
You alone are inviolate.
Every creature praises you, O Mother of Light!
Be for us, we beseech you, therefore,
our perpetual Intercessor to our Lord, Jesus Christ.

Lent

L.
Hail Mary, the Mother of God!
All
You, in your innocent womb, have embraced the Infinite.
Thanks to you, we worship the Most Holy Trinity.
Thanks to you, the Angels and Archangels rejoice.
Thanks to you, the powers of evil take flight.
Thanks to you, the people turn to penance.
Thanks to you, the only Begotten Son of God has shown the Light to those who walk in darkness and in the shadow of death,
Thanks to you, the Apostles announced salvation to all nations.
Thanks to you, the kings reign by the power of the Holy Trinity.
L.
In all our tribulations and difficulties.
All
Help us, O Most Holy Virgin Mary.
L.
Let us pray:
Almighty Lord and God, protect us by your power throughout the course of this day, even as you have enabled us to begin it: do not let us turn aside to any sin, but let our every thought, word and deed aim at doing what is pleasing in your sight. Through Christ our Lord.
All
Amen.
L.
Angel of God
All
My Guardian dear, to whom God’s love commits me here, ever this day be at my side, to enlighten, to guard, to rule and guide. Amen.

Prayers Before Meditation

L.
Come, Holy Spirit,
All
Fill the hearts of your faithful and enkindle in them the fire of your love.
L.
Send forth your Spirit, and they shall be created.
All
And you shall renew the face of the earth.
L.
Let us pray:
All
O God, who did instruct the hearts of your faithful by the light of the Holy Spirit, grant us in the same Spirit to be truly wise and ever to rejoice in his consolation. Through Christ our Lord.
All
Amen.
L.
Queen of Hermits,
All
Pray for us.
L.
Mother of our Order,
All
Pray for us.

or:

Prayer of St. Augustine

L.
God, Holy Spirit,
All
Breathe in me,
That my thoughts may all be holy.
Act in me, O Holy Spirit,
That my work, too, may be holy.
Draw my heart, O Holy Spirit,
That I love all that is holy.
Strengthen me, O Holy Spirit,
To defend all that is holy.
Guard me, then, O Holy Spirit,
That I always may be holy. Amen
L.
Queen of Hermits,
All
Pray for us.
L.
Mother of our Order,
All
Pray for us.

Daily Consecration to Our Lady of Jasna Gora

L.
Holy Mother of Jasna Gora,
All
Mediatrix of all graces, you are full of grace, goodness and mercy. I consecrate to you all my thoughts, words and actions, my soul and body. I ask your help and especially your prayers for my salvation. Today I consecrate myself to you, Holy Mother, totally, with body and soul amid joys and sufferings to obtain for myself and others your help on this earth and eternal life in heaven. Amen.
