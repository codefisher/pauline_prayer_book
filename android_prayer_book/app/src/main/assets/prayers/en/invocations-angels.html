D. Joined to the Guardian Angels

“Consequently, over and above our Marian devotion, we should take care especially to foster devotion to the Holy Angels” (art. 32). The Pauline tradition and the faith teach us, that liturgy and prayer is a work of the whole Church; both of the pilgrim Church on earth and of the glorious one in heaven. In the Constitution on the Sacred Liturgy of the Second Vatican Council, we read: “In the earthly liturgy we take part in a foretaste of that heavenly liturgy…with the Heavenly Hosts, we sing a hymn of glory to the Lord” (Sacrosanctum Concilium 8).

By honouring our Holy Angels, we want to bring our daily life ever closer to the Kingdom of God. Let us keep in mind that the primary obligation of Angels is to worship God. The liturgical texts constantly remind us of this. We are also encouraged to follow the Holy Angels in our worship and praise of God. We do not add anything to God’s glory. Our praise and worship of God, contribute to our salvation.

1. Invocations to the Holy Angels

Holy Mary, Queen of Angels,
Pray for us.
Holy Angels, our Guardians,
Holy Angels, who sing unceasingly “Holy” to God,
Holy Angels, who illuminate our thoughts with divine light,
Holy Angels, who out of love, watch over people,
Holy Angels, who are sent to help those on the way to salvation,
Holy Angels, who constantly contemplate the face of our heavenly Father,
Holy Angels, who rejoice over every penitent sinner,
Holy Angels, who rescued Lot from among those who were doomed,
Holy Angels, who proclaimed joy and peace at the Nativity of our Lord,
Holy Angels, who attended our Lord in the wilderness,
Holy Angels, who appeared at the tomb,
Holy Angels, who comforted the disciples at the Ascension of our Lord,
Holy Angels, who will separate the good from the evil,
Holy Angels, who present our prayers before the throne of God,
Holy Angels, who lead the souls of the just to heaven,
Holy Angels, who keep guard over nations and countries,
Holy Angels, who deliver people from danger,
We sinners, cry unto you,
Hear us, O Lord.
Through the prayers of our Holy Guardian Angels,
That you may preserve children and youth from sin,
That you may strengthen the fidelity of our Order to its vocation,
That you may protect the Church of Christ from all the attacks of our enemies,
That you may inspire us to a greater devotion to the Holy Angels,
That you may give us the grace of final perseverance,
L.
In the presence of the Angels I will sing to you.
All
And adore before your holy temple.
L.
Let us pray:
O God, who in your inexpressible Providence, deigned to send your Holy Angels as our guardians, we humbly ask you, that we may find in their constant protection our defence, and enjoy their company throughout eternity.
Through Christ our Lord.
All
Amen.

Prayer to the Angels

Holy choirs of Angels! You were the first from among all creatures to recognize the supreme authority of God over you, pay him homage and adore him; teach us to humble ourselves before God’s majesty.

Holy servants of the Lord! You always stand before the throne of God, ready to obey his commands; teach us to remain faithful in fulfilling God’s holy will.

St. Michael the Archangel, prince of the heavenly hosts, who with the cry “Who is like God” defeated Lucifer and his angels; inspire us with a zeal to work for the greater glory of God.

St. Gabriel the Archangel, who announced to the Holy Virgin Mary that she would become the Mother of the Son of God by the power of the Holy Spirit; illuminate our minds, that like Mary, we may accept all revealed truths of God with a deep humility.

St. Raphael the Archangel, who happily guided Tobias and protected him against an evil spirit; be our companion on our earthly pilgrimage to the heavenly homeland.

Holy Angels, who unceasingly contemplate the face of the heavenly Father; teach us to see God and his perfection everywhere.

Holy Angels, who sang the glory of God and announced his peace to the people of good will at the birth of Jesus; bring the peace of Christ to our hearts.

Holy Angels, in whose company, Christ will come to judge the living and the dead; lead us on the way to salvation.

Holy Angels, who stand before the throne of God; present to him our prayers and deeds, that he may kindly accept them, forgive all our sins and grant us the grace to join you in heaven forever and ever. Amen.
