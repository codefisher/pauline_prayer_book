10. An Act of Consecration to Mary the Queen and Protectress of the Order

O Holy Mary, Mother of God, ever Virgin! I Brother N. N. choose you this day for my Mother, Queen and Protectress. I resolve never to abandon you, and I promise to work with every thought, word and action to further your glory. I beg you, to take me as your eternal servant. Be at my side always! Do not forsake me at the hour of my death. Amen.
