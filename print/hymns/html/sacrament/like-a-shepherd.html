Refrain: Like a shepherd, He feeds his flock and gathers the lambs in His arms, Holding them carefully close to His heart, leading them home. 1. Say to the cities of Judah: Prepare the way of the Lord. Go to the mountaintop, lift your voice: Jerusalem, here is your God. Refrain 2. I myself will shepherd them, For others have led them astray. The lost I will rescue and heal their wounds and pasture them, giving them rest. Refrain 3. Come unto me if you are heavily burdened, and take my yoke up on your shoulders. I will give you rest.
