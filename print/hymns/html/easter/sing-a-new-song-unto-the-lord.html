Refrain: Sing a new song unto the Lord; let your song be sung from mountains high. Sing a new song unto the Lord, singing alleluia. 1. Shout with gladness, dance for joy. O come before the Lord. And play for Him on glad tambourines, and let your trumpet sound. Refrain 2. Rise, O children, from your sleep; your Saviour now has come. He has turned your sorrow to joy, and filled your soul with song. Refrain 3. Glad my soul for I have seen the glory of the Lord. The trumpet sounds; the dead shall be raised. I know my Saviour lives.
