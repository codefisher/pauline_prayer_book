1. Make me a channel of your peace Where there is hatred let me bring your love Where there is injury, your pardon Lord And where there's doubt, true faith in you. 2. Make me a channel of your peace Where there's despair in life let me bring hope. Where there is darkness, only light And where there's sadness ever joy. 3. Oh, master grant that I may never seek So much to be consoled as to console, To be understood as to understand, To be loved as to love with all my soul. 4. Make me a channel of your peace It is in pardoning that we are pardoned, In giving to all men that we receive, And in dying that we're born to eternal life.
