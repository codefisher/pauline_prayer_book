1. All that I am, all that I do, All that I'll ever have, I offer now to You.  2. All that I dream, all that I pray, All that I'll ever make, I give to You this day.  3. Take and sanctify these gifts for Your honour, Lord. Knowing that I love and serve You is enough reward.

Usual order of performance: vv. 1, 2, 3, 1, 3, 1.
