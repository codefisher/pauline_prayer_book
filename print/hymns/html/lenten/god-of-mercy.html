1. God of mercy and compassion, Look with pity upon me; Father, let me call Thee Father, ’Tis Thy child returns to Thee. Refrain: Jesus, Lord, I ask for mercy; Let me not implore in vain; All my sins I now detest them, Never will I sin again. 2. By my sins I have deserved Death and endless misery, Hell with all its pains and torments, And for all eternity. Refrain 3. By my sins I have abandon’d Right and claim to Heaven above, Where the saints rejoice for ever In a boundless sea of love. Refrain 4. See our Saviour, bleeding dying, On the Cross of Calvary; To that Cross my sins have nail’d Him, Yet He bleeds and dies for me. Refrain
