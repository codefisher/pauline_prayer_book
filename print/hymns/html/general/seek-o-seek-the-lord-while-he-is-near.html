Refrain: Seek, O seek the Lord, while He is near; Trust Him, speak to Him in prayer, and He will hear. 1. God be with us in our lives, Direct us in our calling; Break the snares the world contrives, Keep us from falling. Refrain 2. God, increase in us the life That Christ by dying gave us. Though we faint with mortal strife, His blood will save us. Refrain 3. Strengthen in our hearts the love We owe to one another. How can we love God above And not our brother? Refrain
