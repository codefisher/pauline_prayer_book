1. Be still, my soul; the Lord is on your side; Bear patiently the cross of grief or pain; Leave to your God to order and provide; In every change He faithful will remain. Be still, my soul; your best, your heavenly, Friend Through thorny ways leads to a joyful end. 2. Be still, my soul; your God will undertake To guide the future as He has the past. Your hope, your confidence, let nothing shake; All now mysterious shall be clear at last. Be still, my soul; the tempests still obey His voice, who ruled them once on Galilee. 3. Be still, my soul, when dearest friends depart And all is darkened in the vale of tears; Then you shall better know His love, His heart, Who comes to soothe your sorrow, calm your fears. Be still, my soul; for Jesus can repay From His own fulness all He takes away. 4.
