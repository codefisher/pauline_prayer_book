1. Praise, my soul, the King of Heaven; To His feet thy tribute bring; Ransomed, healed, restored, forgiven, Who like me His praise should sing? Praise Him, praise Him, alleluia, Praise the everlasting King! 2. Praise Him for His grace and favour To our fathers in distress; Praise Him still the same for ever, Slow to chide, and swift to bless: Praise Him, praise Him, alleluia, Glorious in His faithfulness! 3. Father-like He tends and spares us; Well our feeble frame He knows; In His hands He gently bears us, Rescues us from all our foes: Praise Him, praise Him, alleluia, Widely as His mercy flows! 4. Angels, help us to adore Him, Ye behold Him face to face; Sun and moon, bow down before Him, Dwellers all in time and space: Praise Him, praise Him, alleluia, Praise with us the God of grace!
