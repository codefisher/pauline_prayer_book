1. Will you let me be your servant Let me be as Christ to you Pray that I might have the grace To let you be my servant too. 2. We are pilgrims on a journey We are brothers on the road We are here to help each other Walk the mile and bear the load. 3. I will hold the Christ light for you In the night time of your fear I will hold my hand out to you Speak the peace you long to hear. 4. I will weep when you are weeping When you laugh, I’ll laugh with you I will share your joy and sorrow Till we’ve seen this journey through. 5. When we sing to God in heaven We shall find such harmony Born of all we’ve known together Of Christ’s love and agony. 6.
