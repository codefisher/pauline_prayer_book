1. On Jordan’s bank the Baptist’s cry Announces that the Lord is nigh; Awake and hearken, for he brings Glad tidings of the King of kings. 2. Then cleansed be every heart from sin; Make straight the way of God within, And let each heart prepare a home Where such a mighty guest may come. 3. For You are our salvation, Lord, Our refuge, and our great reward; Without Your grace, we waste away, Like flowers that wither and decay. 4. To heal the sick stretch out Your hand, And bid the fallen sinner stand; Shine forth, and let Your light restore Earth’s own true loveliness once more. 5. To God the Son, all glory be, Whose advent sets His people free; Whom with the Father we adore And Holy Ghost evermore.
