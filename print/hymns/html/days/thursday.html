Thursday — Prayer for Priests

Almighty God, look upon the face of Him who is the eternal High Priest, and have compassion on Your priests in today's world. Remember that they are but weak and frail human beings. Stir up in them the grace of their vocation. Keep them all close to You, so that they may never do anything in the slightest degree unworthy of their sublime vocation.

O Jesus, I pray for Your faithful and fervent priests; for those unfaithful and tepid priests; for those labouring at home or abroad in distant mission fields. I pray for those who are tempted; for those who are lonely and desolate; for those who are young; for those who are dying and for those who are in Purgatory.

But above all, I recommend to you the priests dearest to me. I recommend the priest who baptised me; the priests who absolved me from my sins; the priests at whose Masses I have participated and who gave me Your Body and Blood in Holy Communion; the priests who instructed me or helped me by encouragement. I pray devoutly for all the priests to whom I am indebted in any other way in particular for (Father N.)

O Jesus, keep them all close to your heart, and bless them abundantly in time and in eternity.
Amen.

Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be Most Sacred Heart of Jesus Immaculate Heart of Mary St. Joseph St. Jean Marie Vianney

In the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit. Amen.
