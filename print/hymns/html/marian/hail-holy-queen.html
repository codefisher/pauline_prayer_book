1. Hail! holy Queen enthroned above, O Maria! Hail! Mother of mercy and of love, O Maria! Refrain: Triumph all ye Cherubim, Sing with us ye Seraphim, Heav’n and earth resound the hymn: Salve, Salve, Salve Regina! 2. Our life, our sweetness here below, O Maria! Our hope in sorrow and in woe, O Maria! Refrain 3. To thee we cry poor sons of Eve, O Maria! To thee we sigh, we mourn, we grieve, O Maria! Refrain 4. Turn then most gracious Advocate, O Maria! Tow’rd us thine eyes compassionate, O Maria! Refrain 5. When this our exile is complete, O Maria! Show us thy Son, our Jesus sweet, O Maria! Refrain
